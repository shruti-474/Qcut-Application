# Qcut-Application
Project Title : Smart Salon Seat Booking & Smart Mirror Integration

Project Description : 
            A comprehensive, AI-powered mobile application designed to digitize the salon industry. QCut bridges the gap between salon owners and customers by offering real-time appointment scheduling, salon management tools, and an innovative Augmented Reality (AR) Smart Mirror for virtual hairstyle try-ons.
            
The traditional salon industry often relies on walk-in systems and manual record-keeping, leading to long waiting queues, scheduling conflicts, and customer dissatisfaction. Furthermore, customers often struggle to visualize how a specific haircut will look on them before committing to it.

QCut solves these problems by providing a complete digital ecosystem. It allows customers to discover nearby salons, view transparent pricing, book specific time slots, and use AI to try on hairstyles virtually. For salon owners, it acts as a robust management tool to handle appointments, update services, and optimize their daily workflow.

Modules:
1.For Customers:
-Smart Search & Filtering: Find nearby salons categorized by Men, Women, Kids, or Unisex using Google Maps integration.
-Real-Time Seat Booking: View salon availability (Open/Closed), browse service lists with transparent pricing, and book or cancel time slots seamlessly.
-AI Virtual Hairstyle Try-On (Smart Mirror): Upload a photo or use the live camera to visualize different hairstyles in real-time before booking. Powered by ML Kit,  OpenCV, and TensorFlow.
-Digital History & Billing: Keep track of past appointments, payments, and services availed.
-In-App Chat: Communicate directly with salon owners for queries or special requests.

2.For Salon Owners:
-Dashboard Management: Register the shop and manage operational hours, daily status, and contact details.
-Service & Price Control: Add, update, or remove services and dynamically adjust pricing.
-Appointment Handling: Receive real-time booking requests with the authority to approve or reject them to prevent overbooking.
-Customer Insights: View appointment history and track completed services.

3.For Administrators:
-Centralized Monitoring: A secure admin panel to oversee all registered users, shops, and appointments.
-Platform Integrity: Ability to monitor system performance and remove unauthorized, fake, or inactive accounts.


Tech Stack:-
Frontend:
   -IDE: Android Studio
   -Language: Java, XML
   -UI/UX: Native Android Components, RecyclerViews

Backend & Database:
   -Server: XAMPP (Apache)
   -Scripting: PHP (REST APIs for CRUD operations)
   -Database: MySQL
   -Data Format: JSON

Artificial Intelligence & Machine Learning:
  -Computer Vision: OpenCV (Facial landmark detection)
  -Machine Learning: TensorFlow (Hairstyle recommendation models)
  -Real-Time Overlay: Google ML Kit (Face detection and AR overlays)

Third-Party APIs:
  -Location Services: Google Maps API

Future Scope:
-Integration of secure online payment gateways (e.g., Razorpay, Stripe).
-AI-driven customer-flow forecasting to assist salon owners with staff scheduling.
-Advanced facial-structure analysis for highly personalized hairstyle recommendations.
-Multi-language support for broader accessibility.
